Biography

My research focuses on the inference and reconstruction of complex networks. I am currently working with Prof. Tiago P. Peixoto on these topics in the Inverse Complexity Lab. I also investigate epidemiological processes in networks and nonequilibrium phase transitions & critical phenomena in complex networks.

I received my Ph.D. in Physics in August 2023 at Seoul National University with a thesis titled “Prediction and control of epidemics and avalanche dynamics in complex networks” (supervisor: Prof. Byungnam Kahng).
